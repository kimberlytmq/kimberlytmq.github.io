Honour RollResidential College 4

I was awarded a place on the Honour Roll at Residential College 4 in recognition of my contributions to the college community. During my time there, I took on multiple leadership roles, notably serving as Vice Events Director of the College Student Committee, Head of FABRC4S, the college’s sewing club, Publicity Head for the Peer Student Supporters, and Volunteer Manager for RC4lunteers, the college’s volunteering club.

Golden Glasses AwardSmart Systems Institute, NUS

Our team was awarded the Golden Glasses Award for Most Engaging Presentation during the Third Bootcamp of Future Interaction for Smart Glasses, organised by the Smart Systems Institute, NUS. Over the 6-day bootcamp, we explored innovative uses of smart glasses to address real-world challenges, developing a concept with XREAL smart glasses focused on improving travel safety in unfamiliar environments.

SDG Hack FinalistGlobal Green Economic Foundation

Our team placed in the Top 6 out of 50+ teams at the SDG Open Hack 2024 (NUS Edition), where we developed Calmi, a gamified mental wellness app for university students. Designed to promote early intervention and destigmatize mental health, Calmi features daily streaks, customizable avatars, and sensitive-word detection to foster supportive, accessible care.
