Hi, I'm Mou Qi! Nice to meet you.

I’m a Year 3 Computer Science undergraduate at the National University of Singapore.

Introduction

I originally chose to pursue Computer Science because I wanted to challenge myself and learn something new (coding and development). Along the way, I realised that my true passion lies elsewhere. On campus, I’ve taken on multiple leadership roles and directed a variety of projects, and that’s where I discovered my love for event management.

I thrive on being organised and detail-oriented. From backup plans to logistics, from budgets down to the cent, I make sure every aspect of an event is aligned so that it runs as smoothly as possible. These experiences have inspired me to pursue a career in tech-related project management.

My degree also showed me that Computer Science is much broader than just coding. One of my favourite modules was CS3240: Interaction Design, where I learned about user experience and built UI/UX prototypes using Balsamiq and Figma. Through this, I discovered another strength of mine: visualising the user journey and ensuring that digital products meet user expectations. I’m excited to continue exploring UI/UX design as a possible career path.
