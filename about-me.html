Introduction

I chose to pursue Computer Science because I wanted to challenge myself and gain a strong foundation in technology. Along the way I discovered that my true strengths and passions lie with bridging people, processes and technology. On campus, I’ve taken on multiple leadership roles and directed a variety of projects, and these experiences drew me towards product and project management in tech.

I thrive on being organised and detail-oriented. From backup plans to logistics, from budgets down to the cent, I make sure every aspect of a project is aligned so that it runs as smoothly as possible. These experiences have inspired me to pursue a career in tech-related project management.

At the same time, my degree exposed me to the creative side of technology. One of my favourite modules was CS3240: Interaction Design, where I learned about user experience and built UI/UX prototypes using Balsamiq and Figma. Through this, I discovered my strength in visualising user journeys and designing intuitive digital experiences.

Today, I’m actively seeking roles in UI/UX design, project management, or product management, which are areas where I can combine my technical foundation with my skills in leadership, design thinking, and user-centered problem solving.
